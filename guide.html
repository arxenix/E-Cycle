If the computer is in a proper working condition, you can give it to a charity. Even if the computer is old and slow, it could still be utilized by an individual in need. Another option is to give it to a business and get some money back. Some of these locations in Nashua include:

  • Computers with Causes NH - Charity w/ free pickup
  • Staples - Get money back


Dispose of the computer at one of these locations if it is broken and no longer usable:

  • GreenEarthTech
  • RAR
  • Best Buy Stores
  • Nashua Recycling Center - May cost money


It is recommended to wipe the hard drive of your computer before disposing of the computer at any of these locations. However, make sure to preserve the operating system if you are donating to a charity.

If the phone is in a proper working condition, you should give it to a charity. Some of these options in Nashua include:

  • Bridges NH
  • 911 Cell Phone Bank - free pickup


Dispose of the phone at one of these locations if it is broken and no longer usable:

  • GreenEarthTech
  • RAR
  • Best Buy Stores
  • Nashua Recycling Center - May cost money


It is recommended to perform a factory reset of the phone before disposing of it to ensure that your data does not get stolen, and so that your privacy is maintained. See the following list for instructions on resetting different phones:

  • Android
  • iPhone
  • Blackberry
  • Windows Phone
  • Nokia

If the appliance is in a proper working condition, you should donate it to a charity. Some options close to Nashua include:

  • Habitat for Humanity
  • The Wish Project - Lowell, MA


Dispose of the appliance at one of these locations if it is broken and no longer usable:

  • Nashua Recycling Center - May cost money

It is extremely important to properly dispose of any hazardous materials. Some options in Nashua include:

  • Staples - Get paid
  • Nashua Recycling Center - May cost money
